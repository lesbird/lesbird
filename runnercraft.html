Runnercraft IPA download

Runnercraft is no longer on the App Store but you can grab the IPA here and push it to your device.
Use an IPA installer (such as Cydia Impactor) to push it to your device.
I've had some luck with Cydia Impactor on a non-jailbroken iPad.

Check out the forums for some hints and tips.
