ZILOG MCZ-1/20 and PDS MOD 5

In June 2020 I acquired 2 ZILOG MCZ systems on eBay. Between the two I was able to put together one working unit. I worked on these systems in the past so I had some 8 inch disks for them lying around. Surprisingly the disks are still readable and bootable. I also received original system disks with the purchase.

This system was labeled PDS-MOD-5. It has a 9 slot cage and came with 4 cards installed (MCB, MDC, SIB, Z8000)




MCB CPU CARD

MDC FLOPPY CONTROLLER CARD

SIB MULTI SERIAL PORT CARD

Z8000 16BIT DEV CARD WITH 256K RAM

SHUGART SA801 8INCH FLOPPY DRIVE (DS1)

SHUGART SA801 8INCH FLOPPY DRIVE (DS2)


MCZ1 BOOT UP VIDEO


The 2nd system was labeled MCZ1/20. It has a 9 slot cage and came with 3 cards installed (MCB, MDC, SIB)

MCB CPU CARD

MDC FLOPPY CONTROLLER CARD

SIB MULTI SERIAL PORT CARD

SHUGART SA801 8INCH FLOPPY DRIVE (DS1)
