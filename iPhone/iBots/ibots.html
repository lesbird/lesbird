Instructions: Move your blue sphere around the playfield and try to avoid the iBots (red spheres). Move by touching an empty space next to your blue sphere or touch your blue sphere to not move anywhere. The iBots will move towards you and attempt to avoid the barriers. If an iBot touches a barrier it will be destroyed. Win all iBots are destroyed the game is over and you win. If an iBot touches you the game is over and the iBots win.

When the game is over press the PLAY button to start a new game.
