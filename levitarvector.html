Tips on playing the game



4,294,967,295 levels. There is no end to the universe - each level is guaranteed to be different. Using a specialized seeded random terrain algorithm you will never see the same level twice!

Remember games like Thrust? Gravitar? Lunar Lander?

Thrust your ship in a dynamically generated terrain fighting gravity and random enemies. Collect fuel, find and transport the spheroid and destroy all enemies to complete each level. Lunar lander style terrain, cavern terrain, reactor levels and floating islands.

As you progress and reach certain milestones you will encounter new enemies including flying saucers, swarmers, pulse turrets and more.

Upgrade your ship - add shields, dual cannons, reverse cannons.
Buy powerful weapons including smart bombs, laser beams and ...death blossom?

Save the universe. Become the all-time leaderboard champion.

Works with iCade & compatible controllers.
Works with MFI controllers (standard and extended).
